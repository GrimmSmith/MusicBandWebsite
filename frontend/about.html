Our Story

Formed in 2020, Band Name blends rock, soul, and electronic vibes to create a sound that’s both nostalgic and fresh. With roots in [City], we’ve toured across continents and built a loyal fanbase through raw energy and heartfelt lyrics.

Members

  • Sam – Bass
  • Lena – Synth & Backing Vocals

Influences

Inspired by legends like Radiohead, Florence + The Machine, and Daft Punk, our music explores themes of love, loss, and liberation.
